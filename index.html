Welcome to emoji themed game where you can relax and switch off from day to day routine. Your task is to match pairs of emojis behind the cards in the shortest time possible and with the fewest amount of moves. Don't worry if You do not match them up, cards will turn back and You will have another go. All you need to do is remember where the images are for your next move.

1. Press the start button to start the timer and begin the game.
2. Press the reset button if you want to reset the game and start again.

🐸 Sit back, relax and enjoy your game! 🦩
